Target Market

Emergency services

The many emergency services we have set up in our country would take advantage of this technology, the ambulance have
many used for the technology, firefighters would get drones to scout out and put out fires without risking human life, and
first responders would use drones to find survivors faster and get the necessary supplies to them easier. Overall if they are
all on the same network they can be organized and keep track of what's going on and the information they need to complete their
jobs with the most efficiency.

Where?

Emergency services across the country and the government funding behind them would pay for these upgrades. If agencies
across the nation get these upgrades everyone could be better connected.
