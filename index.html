What is 5G?

5G is the 5th generation cellular network technology that creates bigger channels
and lowers latency, opening up the door to many more options beyond a faster
connection for our web browsing and Tv streaming needs.

How could this help us?

The lower latency rates, faster speeds, and ability to connect to more devices
makes many new ideas possible. Self driving cars could communicate with each other
on the road to increase safety, factory robots could become more efficient, farmers
could use drones to water and plant new crops, and emergency services can connect and
use new technology possible with 5G.
